Our story

About Colourfull Corner

From a spare-room hobby to a Facebook shop hundreds of people shop from every month.

It started with leftover thread.

Colourfull Corner began in 2021 with a box of fabric off-cuts too colourful to throw away. What was meant to be a handful of tote bags for friends turned into a Facebook page, then a proper little catalogue, and now this website — but the spare-room spirit never left.

We still design every collection around colour first: the kind that makes a plain outfit or a quiet room feel a little more like you. Everything is made, checked and packed by our small team before it goes out the door.

What we believe in

  • Colour is not extra. It's the whole point — we build every product around a palette, not the other way round.
  • Small batches, less waste. We buy fabric and materials as we need them, not by the pallet.
  • You'll always reach a person. Every message on Facebook or WhatsApp is answered by someone on the team.
